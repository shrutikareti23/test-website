About Us — Our Story

We Were Finance People
Who Got Fed Up

MeeruAI didn't start in a dorm room or a garage. It started in a conference room at 11pm, surrounded by spreadsheets, empty coffee cups, and the quiet frustration of a team that knew there had to be a better way.

Meet the Team Join Us

The Spreadsheet That Broke the Camel's Back

In 2020, our co-founders Anika Sharma and Ravi Mehta were finance leaders at a fast-growing Series B company. Both had come up through Big Four accounting and had spent over a decade building financial models, closing the books, and creating board reports. They were good at their jobs. But they were exhausted.

Every month-end looked the same: 72 hours of frantic reconciliation, version-controlled spreadsheets with names like "Budget_FINAL_v3_REAL_USE_THIS_ONE.xlsx," and a constant, low-level anxiety about whether the numbers were actually right. They managed a team of six smart, ambitious analysts — all of whom spent the majority of their time on data wrangling rather than actual analysis.

"We kept hiring more analysts," Anika recalls, "but the problem didn't get smaller. It just meant we had more people doing the same manual work. I realized we weren't a finance team — we were a very expensive data pipeline."

Ravi had been quietly building automation scripts on the side for years — Python tools to pull data from their ERP, macros to auto-fill report templates. They worked. But they were fragile, required constant maintenance, and terrified the rest of the team. What they needed wasn't a clever hack — they needed a platform built from the ground up for how modern finance actually works.

So in January 2021, they quit their jobs and built one.

Not a Tool Company. A Finance Company.

The early days of MeeruAI were defined by one rule: never build a feature that a finance professional didn't ask for. Anika and Ravi spent the first six months doing nothing but customer discovery — interviewing controllers, CFOs, FP&A managers, and finance directors at companies of every size and shape. They heard the same pain over and over, just with different logos on the door.

The first version of MeeruAI was ugly. It barely had a UI. But it automated reconciliation accurately, and it saved the first pilot customer — a 150-person SaaS company — three full days of controller time per month. That was all the proof they needed.

Company Timeline

Four Years, 500+ Customers

A lot has changed since that conference room in 2020. Here's how MeeruAI grew from an idea into a platform trusted by hundreds of finance teams.

2021
January 2021

MeeruAI is Founded

Anika Sharma and Ravi Mehta leave their finance roles and incorporate MeeruAI. The first line of code is written in Ravi's apartment. They close their first three pilot customers within 60 days — all from their personal networks.

2022
Q2 2022

First 50 Customers — and a Seed Round

MeeruAI reaches 50 paying customers and closes a $3M seed round led by Horizon Ventures. The company doubles headcount from 8 to 18 people, expanding the engineering and customer success teams to support rapid growth.

2023
Q1 2023

Series A — $9M to Accelerate the Platform

MeeruAI closes a $9M Series A, bringing total funding to $12M. The round is used to build out the AI forecasting engine, launch native integrations with NetSuite and SAP, and expand the team to 40 people across 12 countries.

2024
Q4 2024

500+ Customers and Growing Fast

MeeruAI surpasses 500 customers across North America, Europe, and APAC. The platform processes over $40B in transactions annually and saves customers a collective 2 million hours of manual finance work per year. The mission is just getting started.
