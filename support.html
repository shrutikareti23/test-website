Frequently Asked Questions

The answers to the questions our support team hears most often. Click any question to expand the answer.

Most customers are fully set up and syncing live data within a single business day. The initial onboarding involves authenticating your accounting system, mapping your chart of accounts, and running an initial historical sync. Our onboarding team guides you through every step — no engineering help needed on your side.

MeeruAI has native integrations with QuickBooks Online, QuickBooks Desktop, NetSuite, Sage Intacct, SAP S/4HANA, Xero, Microsoft Dynamics 365, Workday Financial Management, and 40+ additional systems. We also offer a REST API and webhook system for custom integrations. If you don't see your system listed, contact us — we add new integrations regularly based on customer demand.

Security is non-negotiable for us. MeeruAI is SOC 2 Type II certified, uses 256-bit AES encryption for all data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit, and undergoes annual penetration testing by an independent third party. We never share your data with third parties and support GDPR, CCPA, and other regional data privacy regulations. You can revoke our access to any connected system at any time.

MeeruAI monitors every sync automatically and will alert you immediately if a sync fails or if data looks anomalous. Most sync issues are resolved automatically by our retry system. If a manual fix is needed, our support team responds to sync-related issues within 2 hours during business hours. You can also view full sync logs in your admin dashboard at any time.

Yes — all MeeruAI plans include unlimited team member seats. You can set granular role-based permissions for each user: full admin, finance manager, read-only viewer, approver-only, and custom roles. You can also invite external collaborators (like your external auditors or bookkeeper) with restricted access to specific views or reports.

You can change or cancel your MeeruAI plan at any time from your Account Settings page — no phone calls required. Annual plans are non-refundable after the first 30 days, but we are happy to work with you if your circumstances change. Monthly plans can be cancelled at any time and take effect at the end of the current billing period. Contact billing@meeruai.com for any billing-related questions.
