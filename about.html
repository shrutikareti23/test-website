Who We Are

Built by Finance Pros, for Finance Pros

We believe financial intelligence should be accessible to every company — not just those who can afford a Big Four consulting team.

🎯

Our Mission

To eliminate the manual, repetitive work that consumes finance teams — and replace it with intelligent automation that gives every finance professional time to think strategically.

🔭

Our Vision

A world where every business, regardless of size, has access to the same financial intelligence and tools as the world's most sophisticated companies.

🌱

Our Culture

💎

Integrity First

🤝

Customer Obsession

Our Journey

How We Got Here

From a small team with a big frustration to a platform trusted by 500+ finance teams worldwide.

1

The Problem

Our founders spent years in corporate finance watching talented professionals drown in spreadsheet maintenance instead of driving strategy.

2

The Insight

Existing tools were built for accountants, not finance leaders. They automated data entry but left all the intelligence work to humans. We saw a different path.

3

The Build

In 2021, we built MeeruAI with one principle: every feature must save real time for real finance professionals. No vanity features, no feature bloat.

4

The Impact

Today, 500+ teams close faster, forecast more accurately, and report with greater confidence — because they spend their energy on decisions, not data wrangling.
